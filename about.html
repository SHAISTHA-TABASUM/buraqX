Who We Are

We help farmers finish important field work more easily

Buraq Agri-Tech helps farmers spray crops faster, reduce medicine waste, and get work done on time with reliable drone services.

Our goal is simple: make spraying and crop checking easier for farmers so they can spend less time worrying about labor, cost, and delays.

M

Mission

  • Help farmers protect crops on time without depending on large labor teams.
  • Reduce spraying cost by using less chemical and finishing work faster.
  • Offer farm services that are easy to book and easy to understand.
V

Vision

  • Make better farm service available to small and large growers alike.
  • Give farmers a faster and safer option than manual spraying.
  • Become a trusted partner farmers can call whenever they need support.
