About

Buraq Learning turns agri-tech knowledge into simple, useful action

We created Buraq Learning to make drone education easy to understand, easy to access, and directly useful in the field for farmers, village entrepreneurs, and agri-support teams.

Every session focuses on real crop work, safe practice, and the confidence needed to use modern tools without confusion.

Learning Topics

Core topics that help learners understand modern drone work from basics to field execution

01

Drone Basics

Understanding drone parts, controls, batteries, and the basic setup needed before field work begins.

02

Spraying Workflows

How to plan a spraying route, calibrate work, and complete jobs with cleaner and more consistent coverage.

03

Safety and Care

Safe chemical handling, field checks, weather awareness, and equipment care for reliable operation.

04

Crop Observation

Simple ways to inspect crop condition, notice stress points, and report what is happening in the field.

05

Mapping and Planning

How field mapping supports route planning, smart coverage, and better preparation for each workshop task.

06

Operator Readiness

Hands-on practice that helps learners move from watching a demo to completing guided operations confidently.
